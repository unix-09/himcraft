About the Product

A timeless piece crafted in the heart of Himachal. Handwoven by skilled artisan Ramchand Ji, this muffler features the iconic Kullu Patti design inspired by Himalayan traditions. Made from 100% pure wool, it offers warmth, comfort, and a touch of elegance — perfect for everyday wear or special occasions.
